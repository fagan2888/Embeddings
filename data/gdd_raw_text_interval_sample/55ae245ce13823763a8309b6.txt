Map of Structural Features in Part of Central Kentucky^ by
Douglas F. B. Black, 1970
The accompanying map was compiled from 30 published and unpublished geologic quadrangle maps prepared under the cooperative geologic mapping program of the Kentucky Geological Survey and U. S. Geological Survey. Structure contours of the source maps were drawn on more than one stratigraphic horizon; so contours that were not drawn on top of the Tyrone Limestone (Middle Ordovician) were converted to that horizon for this map.
Published reports by the following authors were used in the compilation:
Black (1964, 1967, 1968), Cressman (1964, 1965, 1967, 1968), Greene (1965, 1966a, b), Kanizay and Cressman (1967), MacQuown (1968a, b), MacQuown and Dobrovolny (1968), Miller (1967), Pomeroy (1968), Simmons (1967), Weir (1967, 1969) and Wolcott (1969).
Several unpublished source maps were made by authors listed above; others were prepared by J. W. Allingham, L. D. Harris, F. R. Shawe and P. B. Wigley.
